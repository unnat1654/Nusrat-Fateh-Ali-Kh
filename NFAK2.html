Ustaad Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan








Personalia: Real name  :   Pervez Fateh Ali Khan (13 October 1948 – 16 August 1997)
Born  :   (13 October 1948) Faisalabad, Pakistan
Died  :   (16 August 1997) London, England, U.K.




He was the fifth child and first son of Fateh Ali
Khan, a musicologist, vocalist, instrumentalist,
and Qawwal. Khan's family included four older
sisters and a younger brother, Farrukh Fateh Ali
Khan. Khan sahab grew up in central Faisalabad.
The tradition of Qawwali in the family had passed
down through successive generations for almost
600 years.





Initially, his father did not want Khan to follow
the family's vocation. He had his heart set on
Nusrat choosing a much more respectable career
path and becoming a doctor or engineer because
he felt Qawwali artists had low social status.
However, Khan showed such an aptitude for and
interest in Qawwali, that his father finally
relented. He began by learning the tabla before
moving on to vocals.
